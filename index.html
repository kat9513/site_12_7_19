Get to know Katie

Katie Higgins received her MBA in 2017 from Austin College in Sherman, TX shortly after spending a semester studying Renaissance Art at Florence University of Art in Italy. Her work is informed and inspired by the Japanese art collective Kaikai Kiki, Italian Baroque Masters, and Cartoons.

She strives to combine both realism and fantasy in her paintings, often opting for garishly bright color palettes. Her subjects include abstract organic shapes, the female form, and floral arrangements, often wavering between reverie and nightmare.

Katie’s work has been described as “rainbow disembowelment” and “cutesy supernatural body horror.”

She is a Lubbock native and now works as a Digital Marketing Specialist in Houston, TX.
